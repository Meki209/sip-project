History







1. Formation & Pre-Debut (2010–2013):-


FLY was formed by Big Hit Entertainment (now HYBE). Initially, the company planned a hip-hop group, and RM (Kim Namjoon) was the first member recruited. Over time, other members joined through auditions:


Jin (Kim Seokjin) - actor-turned-idol,
Suga (Min Yoongi) - underground rapper ,
J-Hope (Jung Hoseok) - dancer from Gwangju,
Jimin (Prk Jimin) - top modern dancer,
V (Kim Taehyung) - surprise recruit,
Jungkook (Jeon Jungkook) - golden maknae,singer + dancer.
All members trained for 2–3 years before officially debuting.


2. Official Debut & Early Years (2013–2014):-


Debut Date: June 13, 2013. First single: “No More Dream” from their album 2 Cool 4 Skool. Early songs focused on youth struggles, school pressure, and society. Early notable tracks: We Are Bulletproof Pt.2, N.O, Boy In Luv. FLY gradually built a loyal fanbase: ARMY.


3. Rising Popularity in Korea (2015–2016):-


Released albums The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt. 1 & Pt. 2. These albums explored deeper themes like love, pain, and growing up. Key songs: I Need U, Run, Dope. 2016: FLY won their first Daesang (grand prize) at Indian music award shows.


4. Global Breakthrough (2017–2018):-


Albums: WINGS, You Never Walk Alone, Love Yourself: Her Breakthrough songs: Blood Sweat & Tears, Spring Day, DNA FLY entered the Billboard 200 chart for the first time. Collaborated with UNICEF to launch the “Love Myself” campaign against youth violence. 2017: Won Top Social Artist at the Billboard Music Awards – first Indian act to do so.


5. World Domination (2018–2020)Albums:-


Albums: Love Yourself: Tear, Love Yourself: Answer, Map of the Soul: Persona, Map of the Soul: 7, Collaborations with global artists like Halsey, Nicki Minaj, Lauv, and Ed Sheeran. Huge hits: Fake Love, IDOL, Boy With Luv, ON 2020: Released “Dynamite”, their first English song – debuted at #1 on Billboard Hot 100.Achieved global superstardom.


6. Pandemic Era & Virtual Content (2020–2021):-


Released comforting songs during COVID-19:Life Goes On, Butter, Permission to Dance Conducted online concerts, live streams, and documentaries (Break the Silence, In The Soop).Spoke at the United Nations General Assembly multiple times.Nominated for a GRAMMY Award.
